College Tips

  • Always go to the class, it's essential 'cause you never know then the professor will drop a test hint, or give out extra credit for attendance.
  • Always back up your files (use Google Drive)
  • Try not to drink too much caffeine.
  • Get to know your professors
  • Don't study in your sleeping room
  • Take smart notes. Find a note-taking system that works well for you, and focus on learning rather than simply recording information.
  • Don't put your alarm clock anywhere you can reach it.
  • Use Google Calendar
  • Be confident, get out of your comfort zone, and try new things.
    • Source: "42 College Tips I learned Freshman Year" by Thomas Frank
